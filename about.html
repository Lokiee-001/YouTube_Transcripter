Our Mission

In today's information-rich world, video content has become a primary source of knowledge and entertainment. However, the time required to consume this content often exceeds what busy individuals can spare.

SummifyAI was founded in 2022 with a simple goal: to develop AI-powered tools that extract the essence of video content, delivering key insights in a fraction of the time.

Our technology is particularly valuable for students, professionals, and lifelong learners who need to stay informed but face time constraints.
